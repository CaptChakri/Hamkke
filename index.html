Trust & safety

Meeting a stranger? We've got you.

Every layer of Hamkke is built so you can show up confident — whether it's your first session or your hundredth.

Government ID verification

Real people only. Every profile confirms a government ID before they can match.

Gym check-in confirmation

We confirm members actually train where they say — no catfishing your squat rack.

Member vouches & reviews

See honest ratings and vouches from other lifters before you ever say hello.

Meet during staffed hours

For first sessions, Hamkke suggests public, staffed gym times — never an empty room.

Share your live session

Send a trusted contact your live location and session details in a single tap.

Certified coaches

Paid mentors display real coaching and CPR / first-aid credentials, verified by us.
