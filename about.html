About

Never lift alone.

Hamkke means “together” in Korean. We’re building a safer way to find gym buddies and mentors nearby.

Getting into the gym is easier when someone is expecting you. Hamkke helps beginners learn from experienced lifters, regulars find consistent training partners, and mentors support people a few steps behind them.
